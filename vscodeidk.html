To take care of a bunny you need:

  • A spacious cage - A cage is needed to provide a rabbit with some sort of shelter or place they feel safe in and can go to sleep, eat and other necessities



  • Proper food and water - As alwasy all animals need food and water, however some rabbits have specific diets and in general, bunnies CAN NOT eat everything

    • Click here for great rabbit food brands!

  • Space to run around - Bunnies should be let out once in a while from their cages, they dont need to be led out outside but around the room their cage is in

  • Most importantly they need love!!! - Bunnies need a loving and committed home to be in, it will also be reflected in their behaviour around their family/owner
